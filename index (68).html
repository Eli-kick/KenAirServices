About Ken Air Services

Honest Work. Dependable Service.

Founded in 2005 by Ken Grice, Ken Air Services has been a trusted name in HVAC and refrigeration along the Gulf Coast for two decades — backed by Ken's nearly four decades of hands-on experience in the trade. Our family-owned company has grown the old-fashioned way: through honest work, repeat customers, and word-of-mouth referrals from neighbors.

We're not here to upsell you or pressure you into services you don't need. We show up, diagnose the issue, explain your options clearly, and focus on doing the job right the first time. That's how we've built a reputation our community trusts.
