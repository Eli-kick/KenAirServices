About Ken Air Services

Honest Work. Dependable Service.

Ken Air Services has been a trusted name in HVAC and refrigeration along the Gulf Coast since 2003. Founded by Ken Grice, our family-owned company has built its reputation one job at a time — through quality workmanship, fair pricing, and treating every customer like a neighbor.

Whether you need a quick repair, a full system replacement, or routine maintenance to keep things running smoothly, we're here to help. Licensed in both Alabama and Mississippi, we proudly serve homes and businesses across the region with the same care we'd give our own.
